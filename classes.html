Classes

Beginners

Newbies to yoga workouts should plan to log two or three yoga sessions per week to start. This will ensure that your body gets used to the stretches and poses that you'll be working on as you move forward in your journey.

MWF : 8AM to 9AM TTHS : 10AM-11AM

Intermediate

You should be comfortable flowing through poses and connecting your breath to your movement. In these classes, you’ll start to work on more challenging poses and inversions. You’ll also learn how to be more in tune with your own body and what it’s capable of.

MWF : 10AM-11AM TTHS : 1PM-2PM

Advanced

It is a science that, when fully practiced both on and off the yoga mat, can enhance and advance your life. Far from the Western approach of yoga which is mainly asana-focused, the full practice will support developing clearer understanding of yourself and the world around you.

MWF : 1PM-2PM TTHS : 8AM to 9AM
